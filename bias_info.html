How XP works: You earn 10–25 XP per sentence based on how much time you spend reviewing it. The required time starts at 25 seconds and increases by 5 s for each type in "Quite certain" and 10 s for each type in "Uncertain". A thoughtful explanation earns +15 bonus XP. Consecutive saves build a streak (3×: +10 %, 5×: +25 %, 10×: +50 %). Your XP level tracks your overall contribution — from 🌱 Beginner Annotator up to 💎 Bias Legend.

How to participate

Try selecting the fitting bias types yourself! Review random sentences from our dataset. Remove types from "Quite certain" if you disagree, add types that are not yet selected, and make a final decision for every type listed under "Uncertain".

Main task

Decide for every type listed under Uncertain whether it really applies or not.
